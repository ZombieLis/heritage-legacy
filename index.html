The Five Core Rules
I
Rule I
The Family Name
Your trade becomes your surname. Bakers. Fletchers. Coopers. This name never changes — regardless of heir gender.
II
Rule II
The Family Trade
Every heir must follow the founder's career. Start as an apprentice or market stall seller in Gen 1. Own a family business by Gen 5.
III
Rule III
The Trade Heir Rule
The heir is never born — they earn it. The child who shows the greatest aptitude and passion for the family trade inherits everything.
IV
Rule IV
The Family Home
Start on the largest lot with §30,000. Empty lot only. Never move. Never sell. Watch a humble beginning grow into a grand estate.
V
Rule V
The Heirloom Rule
The founder creates one heirloom tied to their trade — never sold. Each heir adds their own. Every heir keeps a personal journal from childhood, written at least once per in-game week. Journals are passed down and eventually housed in the Family Museum.

The Family Museum
Your legacy made physical.
GENS 1–3 · THE HERITAGE ROOM
One dedicated room in the family home. Heir portraits, heirlooms and journals displayed with intention. Visitors should understand the family's history at a glance.
GENS 4–5 · THE FAMILY MUSEUM
Expands to a community lot open to the neighbourhood. Houses every heir portrait, all heirlooms, family journals, the Family Chronicle, and all dynasty achievements.
